A few words about the author…

The first attempts appeared somewhere in the middle-end of the last year of the
secondary school, but more mature and serious stuff - after the army. Part of the
verses - they are songs (meaning, every verse has music, recorded by guitar
accords). The author's songs sounded among the participants of the region 'KSP'
(Amateur Song Club) and his friends.

Most of the verses are written in Russian and all of them are divided to a cycles (usually
the writing period and location). Each page contains
an esoteric acronym
and an author's logo (top right) .

The author would be very happy to discuss these projects with readers, redactor and
just interested persons (you can see there such projects).
